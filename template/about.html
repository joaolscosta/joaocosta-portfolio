INTERNSHIPS

IST - Assembly and Monitoring of autonomous vehicles

During my internship at IST, I had the opportunity to work on assembling and monitoring autonomous vehicles, specifically a small robot called "Rasteirinho." Under the guidance of a university professor, I completed various courses aimed at understanding its components and operations. The robot, powered by two DC motors controlled by an Arduino nano, was equipped with a computer on top. My tasks included navigating predefined paths based on initial instructions, operating the robot via Bluetooth, and implementing a camera to follow black lines.
