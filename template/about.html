About

Hi! My name is João Costa. I am 22 years old and recently graduated in 2024 with a degree in Computer and Informatics Engineering from Instituto Superior Técnico.

My love for computer engineering began in high school. I was always fascinated by technology and how things work. When I first started college, I chose to study Electrical Engineering. But after my first year, I realized that my true passion was in computer science, so I decided to switch majors. This was the best decision I made because I enjoyed every moment of my studies in Computer and Informatics Engineering.

Apart from my studies, I love playing sports. Being active helps me stay healthy and energetic. I have a variety of hobbies that I enjoy in my free time, such as reading, gaming, and learning new skills. These hobbies help me relax and have fun.

I also spend time working on personal projects. These projects allow me to apply what I have learned and explore new ideas. I am very proud of these projects because they show my creativity and dedication.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I am excited to share my work and experiences with you. I hope you enjoy exploring my projects as much as I enjoyed creating them!
